\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
2D point on a sphere 

This class represents a point on a sphere. Its coordinates are measured in degrees of longitude and latitude.

Distances between {\ttfamily Terrestrial\+Points} are measured in kilometers. Speeds between two {\ttfamily Terrestrial\+Trajectory\+Points} will be measured in kilometers per hour.
