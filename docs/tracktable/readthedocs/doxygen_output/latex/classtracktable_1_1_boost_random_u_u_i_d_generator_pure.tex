\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Wraps a boost uuid pure generator to act as a Random\+UUIDGenerator. 

The boost uuid pure generator is a lighter weight generator with less entropy, but as stated in the boost documentation it "{}will satisfy the majority of use cases."{}
