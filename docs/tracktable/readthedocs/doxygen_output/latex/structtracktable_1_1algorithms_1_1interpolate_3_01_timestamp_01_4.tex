\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Interpolate between two timestamps. 

Standard linear interpolation. At {\ttfamily t \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}= 0} you get back the first value. At {\ttfamily t \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}= 1} you get the second value. Anywhere in between, you get {\ttfamily (1-\/t) \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} first + t \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} second}.
